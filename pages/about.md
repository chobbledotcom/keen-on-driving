      ## Training & Mentorship


      Linda Edwards has a wealth of experience in teaching potential driving
      instructors and uses a mixture of teaching techniques including
      observations of her pupils' lessons, one-to-one classroom sessions, group
      classroom sessions and in-car training. She ensured that Daniel not only
      passed his ADI Part 3 but also acquired the skills necessary to understand
      why he passed. It is this deeper understanding that motivates Daniel every
      day to get the best out of his pupils.


      Whilst training, Daniel was mentored by Andy Scanlon from Scan School of
      Motoring. Andy, after a successful career in swimming coaching, followed
      in his dad's footsteps to become a driving instructor and guided Daniel
      through everything he needed to know. With only around 35% of instructors
      passing the final test nationally, it was challenging at times, but Andy
      always found a way to keep Daniel motivated. These are experiences Daniel
      now passes on to his pupils to give them every chance of success.

      ## Teaching All Abilities


      With Scan School of Motoring, Daniel gained experience teaching a wide
      variety of pupils:


      *   **Confident drivers** who are raring to go

      *   **Nervous pupils** who sometimes need gently persuading to sit in the
      driver's seat for the first time

      *   Pupils with **severe anxiety**

      *   Pupils with **ADHD**

      *   Pupils with **autism**


      Daniel is happy to take each lesson at a pace suited to each individual
      pupil.
    right_content: >-
      ## Finding the Balance


      Daniel understands that all pupils learn differently.


      The more confident drivers can be more likely to take risks, whereas less
      confident drivers can at times create risks from driving too slow.


      Daniel likes to think that once his pupils are test ready, they have all
      met at that middle point — below over-confidence but with enough
      self-belief in their ability.
